Sponsor packages

Why sponsor Drupalaton?

Drupalaton is the official DrupalCamp in Hungary, being organized for the seventh time, and for the fourth time it's a 100% international event. 
Having workshops and trainings in English, we open the doors for everyone interested in Drupal. Our goal is to broaden the knowledge of the attendees in different fields, deepen connections among people already using Drupal, provide personal and professional networking opportunities and also resources on how to use Drupal in general. During these days we expect to have more than 120 attendees to talk about front-end, business and anything that is related to Drupal.

Drupalaton is organized at Balatonalmádi, a pretty and cosy lakeside town located on the northeast corner of Balaton, the biggest warm-water lake in Europe. This is the perfect opportunity to connect work and holiday!

If you would like to sponsor Drupalaton as an attendee, you have the possibility to choose our individual sponsor ticket.
