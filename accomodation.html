Accomodation

You have various choices to stay while at Drupalaton, but here are some recommendations. The event venue is Hotel Ramada, so you might want to stay close ;)

Ramada AURORA Hotel & Resort Lake Balaton****

In 2016 Ramada Aurora provides the perfect venue for Drupalaton. The hotel is located a few hundred meters away from Lake Balaton, with direct connection to the pier and the promenade. The hotel's own beach is accessible in 5 minutes, the city centre with bars and restaurants is in similar distance. The hotel offers awesome wellness services as well. For more information please visit their website. 

Please send your reservation email to reservation@ramadabalaton.hu with the following subject: 'Drupalaton room reservation' to get special discount prices for the period of 11-14 August 2016.

„Drupalaton” room rates

  • Standard single room: 23 400 HUF/night/room (approx. 75 €)
  • Standard single with panorama on the lake: 25 400 HUF/night/room  (approx. 82 €)
  • Standard twin/double room: 36 900 HUF/night/room (approx. 120 €)
  • Standard twin/double with panorama on the lake: 38 900 HUF/night/room( approx. 125 €)
  • NOTE: The prices in EUR are only for guidance, you'll need to pay in HUF.

The price does not include the 460 HUF/night/person tourist tax above the age of 18 and the 990 HUF/car/night parking fee (or 1990 HUF/car/night in the underground garage).

The price does include the followings:
room with buffet breakfast and dinner, use of the swimming pool, jacuzzi, wellness department, sauna and fitness room, free WIFI in rooms, room safe, VAT (Value Added Tax)
