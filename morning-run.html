Good morning, runners!

No matter if you are a regular runner or just don’t want to get out of shape during your well deserved summer DrupalCamp, we have a great idea to start your mornings at Drupalaton.

Join the Drupal runner team for an easy run in front of Hotel Ramada at 8:00am on Friday and Saturday morning! You’ll surely enjoy the 5 km distance on the shore of Lake Balaton (the trip is approx. 45 min long). Expected pace: approx. 6:00 - 6:30 minutes per km.

During the run we'll pass by some famous sights of Balatonalmádi, including chx's house :)

Let us know at the registration desk in the venue if you are interested.
